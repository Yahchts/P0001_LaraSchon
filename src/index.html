Why Lara Schön?

Since our founding in 2013, we have been guided by one principle: the desire to provide everyone with effective and safe skin care solutions.

Our skincare products are formulated to work with all skin types, not against them. Whether you're worried about dark circles under your eyes, hyperpigmentation, acne, redness, fine lines and wrinkles or dehydration, our luxurious cruelty-free skincare cosmetics delivers real results without side effects.

Reputation

The key principle of Lara Schön is to enhance cosmetic products quality and effectiveness as well as implement the continuous improvement strategy. Lara Schön, the brand founder & owner of 2 cosmetic brands Lara Schön & Schön Berg, believes that she is personally responsible to each and every customer for the quality of manufactured skin care products.
