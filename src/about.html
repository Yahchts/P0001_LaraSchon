MEET THE FOUNDER Who We Are

Since our founding in 2013, we have been guided by one principle: the desire to provide everyone with effective and safe skin care solutions.

Our skincare products are formulated to work with all skin types, not against them. Whether you're worried about dark circles under your eyes, hyperpigmentation, acne, redness, fine lines and wrinkles or dehydration, our luxurious cruelty-free skincare cosmetics delivers real results without side effects.

All of our skincare products are free from parabens, mineral oil, sodium lauryl sulphate, artificial colours and fragrances. We are 100% cruelty-free - with no exceptions.

In our skin care products we use the highest quality ingredients and unique technologies that enable to keep their valuable properties.

Floral waxes, phytoestrogens, plant stem cells, seaweed and snow algae complexes, enzymes, encapsulated collagen and elastin, vitamins, bioengineered growth factors, floral and plant essential oils – all the ingredients originate from eco-friendly areas. We select the suppliers that comply with environmentally conscious methods of plant growing and green production process flow.

We highly appreciate Your choice of our skin care products.

Caring of your beauty and youth.
