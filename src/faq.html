Frequently Asked Questions
  • WHAT IS COUNTERFEIT?
    Counterfeit products come from unscrupulous businessmen and individuals. Products purchased outside of Lara Schön's professional channel come from unknown sources outside the authorized distribution chain.
    WHAT IS THE DANGER OF COUNTERFEIT?
    Counterfeit products may cause irritation or even infection. They may not be safe to use.
    WHAT IS LARA SCHÖN DOING TO FIGHT COUNTERFEIT?
    Lara Schön does everything to stop the sales’ channels of fake Lara Schön products with intelligence and investigative procedures that uncover perpetrators and their tactics, including the misuse of online e-commerce sites.
    HOW CAN I MAKE SURE THAT I AM NOT BUYING FAKE PRODUCTS?
    Lara Schön products are sold ONLY through authorized professional partners. Our professional partners are specially trained to recommend products based on your skin's needs.
    UNAUTHORIZED SITES
    Counterfeiters often sell their fake products through online stores. Products purchased from these sites are counterfeit. Lara Schön cannot guarantee their quality or authenticity and accepts no responsibility for them.
    The following websites have not been authorized by Lara Schön to retail our products and are in no way associated with Lara Schön's products and brand:
